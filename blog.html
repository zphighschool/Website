2. Independence Day

Z.P. High School, Sawargaon celebrated Independence Day with pride and patriotism. The program began with the hoisting of the National Flag followed by the National Anthem. Students presented patriotic songs, speeches, and cultural performances to honor the freedom fighters. The atmosphere was filled with the spirit of unity and devotion to the nation.

3. Republic Day

The program began with the hoisting of the National Flag by the chief guest, followed by the National Anthem. Students showcased patriotic songs, dances, and speeches highlighting the importance of the Constitution and the sacrifices of our leaders. Teachers delivered inspiring messages about unity, equality, and responsibility. The entire school was filled with the spirit of pride and nationalism. The event concluded with the distribution of sweets and a vote of thanks.

4. Yoga Day

Z.P. High School, Sawargaon celebrated Farewell Day with mixed emotions of joy and nostalgia. The event began with a warm welcome to the outgoing students by juniors and teachers.
