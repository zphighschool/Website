1. Teachers Day

2. Independence Day

Z.P. High School, Sawargaon celebrated Independence Day with pride and patriotism. The program began with the hoisting of the National Flag followed by the National Anthem. Students presented patriotic songs, speeches, and cultural performances to honor the freedom fighters. The atmosphere was filled with the spirit of unity and devotion to the nation.

3. Republic Day

The program began with the hoisting of the National Flag by the chief guest, followed by the National Anthem. Students showcased patriotic songs, dances, and speeches highlighting the importance of the Constitution and the sacrifices of our leaders. Teachers delivered inspiring messages about unity, equality, and responsibility. The entire school was filled with the spirit of pride and nationalism. The event concluded with the distribution of sweets and a vote of thanks.

4. Yoga Day

The program began with a brief introduction on the importance of yoga for a healthy body and mind. Teachers and students actively participated in performing various yoga asanas under the guidance of instructors. The celebration emphasized the role of yoga in daily life for stress relief and overall well-being. Students also presented speeches and slogans promoting the theme ‚ÄúYoga for Harmony and Peace.‚Äù The event concluded with a pledge to practice yoga regularly.

5. Shivjayanti

the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, with great pride and devotion. The program began with garlanding the portrait of Shivaji Maharaj and offering tributes. 6. Farewell Day

Z.P. High School, Sawargaon celebrated Farewell Day with mixed emotions of joy and nostalgia. The event began with a warm welcome to the outgoing students by juniors and teachers. Students expressed their memories and gratitude towards the school through speeches, songs, and cultural performances. Teachers shared words of encouragement and blessings for the bright future of the students. Juniors presented gifts and organized fun activities to make the day memorable. The program ended with heartfelt goodbyes, group photographs, and best wishes for the next journey of life.
