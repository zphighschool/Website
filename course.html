Zilla Parishad High School offers various essential facilities to support student learning and development. The school has spacious and well-ventilated classrooms, a library with a collection of educational books, and a science lab for conducting practical experiments. The school ensures safe drinking water, hygienic toilets for boys and girls, and nutritious mid-day meals under the government scheme.
