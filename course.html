Our facilities✨

Zilla Parishad High School offers various essential facilities to support student learning and development. The school has spacious and well-ventilated classrooms, a library with a collection of educational books, and a science lab for conducting practical experiments. It also provides a computer lab for basic computer education. A large playground is available for sports and physical activities. The school ensures safe drinking water, hygienic toilets for boys and girls, and nutritious mid-day meals under the government scheme. For safety, CCTV cameras are installed on the campus, and tree plantations help maintain a clean and green environment.

Library📚

The library at Z.P.H.S. is a quiet and resourceful place where students can read and learn. It has a good collection of textbooks, storybooks, and reference materials to support their studies and improve reading habits.

Playground⛳

The playground at Z.P.H.S. is spacious and well-maintained, providing students a safe space for outdoor games and physical activities. It encourages fitness, teamwork, and overall development.

Mid time meal🍴

Z.P.H.S. provides a nutritious mid-day meal to all students, ensuring they stay healthy and focused on learning. The meals are prepared hygienically and follow the government guidelines.
